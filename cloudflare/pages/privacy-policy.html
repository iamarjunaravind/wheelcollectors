Privacy Policy

At Wheel Collectors, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you make a purchase or register an account, we collect information such as your name, email address, shipping address, and phone number. We do not store your credit/debit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process orders, provide customer support, and send you occasional updates about your orders or promotional offers (if you opted in).

3. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your personal information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services like Razorpay for payment processing and Google Analytics to understand website traffic. These third parties have their own privacy policies.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience, such as remembering items in your cart. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but it may affect some features of our site.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us at vault@wheelcollectors.in.
